Gaming

We will have video games, board games, and table top games for free play from 11am to 8pm. In addition, we will be hosting multiple tournaments throughout the day. Gaming will be located in the Price Center East Ballroom.

Tournament Schedule

All tournaments are held in PC East Ballroom. Sign-ups will be held at the Gaming Table in PC East next to all of the game set-ups. After signing up, please check in at the Gaming Table 10 minutes before the tournament start time. If sign-ups go over the cap, there will be a waitlist.
