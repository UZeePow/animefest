Gaming

We will have video games, board games, and table top games for free play from 11am to 8pm. In addition, we will be hosting multiple tournaments throughout the day. Gaming will be located in the Price Center East Ballroom, ERC College Room, Marshall College Room, and Revelle College Room.

Tournament Descriptions

All games will be available for free play before and after their respective tournament.

Smash 64 Tournament

PC East Ballroom, 12pm - 1pm

In a once in a lifetime opportunity, Cal Animage Beta is bringing Smash 64 to Animefest. Experience an era before wave-dashing and Meta Knight as you fight with an abomination of an apparatus only second to the Virtual Boy – the Nintendo 64 triple-legged controller.

Cap: 16 people

Special Rules: No rules barred. 4 player Smash. Items on.

Street Fighter V Tournament

PC East Ballroom, 1pm - 3pm

It’s time to light punch, light punch, forward, light kick, hard punch some dudes in the latest iteration of Street Fighter. Featuring classic characters and a brand new fighting system, this game will be turning heads with its flashy graphics and dynamic battles.

Cap: 10 people

YuYuHakusho Dark Tournament Tournament

PC East Ballroom, 3pm - 4pm

Due to popular demand, this game is returning for another bout. Featuring more broken characters than your fingers can count, play your old-school favorites like Yusuke and Kurama or forgettable characters like Roto and Chu.

Cap: 16 people

Eternal Fighter Zero Tournament

PC East Ballroom, 4pm - 5pm

With solid fighting mechanics, moe characters, and a strong ongoing fanbase, Cal Animage Beta is excited to bring Eternal Fighter Zero to this year’s Animefest. Featuring characters from Key’s Kanon and Air and visual novels that the founding members worked on in the past, this is a must-play for any person who loves anything that is Key.

Cap: 16 people

Divekick Tournament

PC East Ballroom, 5pm - 5:30pm

Cap: 16 people

Smash 4 Tournament

ERC College Room, 5pm - 8pm

More characters, more stages, more items, more everything, babyyyyyy. It’s time to play the Smash that everyone is still hankering about. Whether you’re a new player or an old-school player, you’ll be enjoying yourself no matter what. We will have controllers available, but you may bring your own controller.

Cap: 32 people

Special Rules: 1v1. No items.

Familiar Works Mahou no Bazooka Demo

PC East Ballroom

Anime OP/ED Karuta

Revelle College Room; 12pm, 1pm, 2pm, 5pm, 6pm (1 hr each)

Confident in recognizing 40 anime openings and endings? Assured of your speed and skill at Karuta? A master of reading upside down words (and rightside up ones too)? Whether you are or aren't amazing at all of these things, come on down to Revelle College Room and participate in Cal Animage Beta's first Anime OP/ED Karuta tournament! Rules will be explained before each tournament; a knowledge of Karuta is not required. View the tracklist here.

Magic the Gathering Draft

Marshall Room, 2pm - 7pm

Smuggler's Copter may be banned, but that doesn't stop you from drafting it! Come to the Marshall College Room at 2pm for an Aether Revolt Draft (2 AER, 1 KLD). Be warned: the draft will likely consume the rest of the time for Animefest, so make sure you take care of any business you have beforehand! Cost is $15, that includes a pack per win prizing. Signups are live here and cap at 8 players.

Persona 5: Break Your Heart Edition

PC East Ballroom, 3pm - 5pm

Ever since Persona 5's release date was pushed back to April, millions of Americans have been mourning this loss. Many have taken day-offs during Valentine's Day to spend quality time with their potential P5 waifus and husbandos. Others have broken up with their girlfriends and boyfriends just to get their hands on P5. However, you are not alone. The Animefest administration understands your pains.
