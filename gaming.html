Gaming

Video games galore! Six video game stations will be available, with WiiU's, GameCubes/Wii's, and a PS2. Free Play will be available for each station when tournaments are not going on, with Super Smash Brothers Melee and Mario Kart Double Dash available specifically on the GameCube/Wii stations.

Gaming Tournaments

Prizes will be awarded for these events!

Super Smash Brothers 4 Tournament

12:00 P.M. - Open Gaming

Settle it in Smash! Animefest will have a SSB4 tournament, hosted on three of our six video game stations. This game is so popular it barely needs explanation beyond that we'll try our best to have the latest DLC characters and stages for the event!

Stream: http://twitch.tv/otaku_al

Brackets: here

Yu Yu Hakusho Dark Tournament

2:00 P.M. - Open Gaming

Hate playing a fighting game where people have extensively researched combos and movelists? That may still happen, but it's unlikely with a game like YuYuHakusho Dark Tournament! Play as your favorite character from the beloved anime and manga YuYuHakusho and compete in an intense tournament for a game barely anyone will be familiar with.

Kirby Air Ride Tournament

4:00 P.M. - Open Gaming

That pink puffball's no Jigglypuff boy, no Jigglypuff! Race with your friends as Kirbies in a myriad of colors and racing stars. A high speed kart racer awaits you, with all of Kirby's copy abilities to blast away the competition and a gliding system so fluid that Mario Kart 7 wishes it could be as good.

Street Fighter V Tournament

4:00 P.M. - Open Gaming

Have a burning urge to defeat players who might be off their game because the game you're playing came out only a couple of days ago? Train your Street Fighter V skills here with our very own Street Fighter V tournament. In contrast to our YuYuHakusho Dark Tournament Tournament, Street Fighter is a quite a popular fighting game - but there will be an equivalent lack of players experienced with the game mechanics. For fighting game fans new and old, this is the time to shine! On an completely unrelated note, the Introduction to Fighting Games panel starts at 12:30 P.M. in Revelle College Room.
