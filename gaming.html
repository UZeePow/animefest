Gaming

We will have video games, board games, and table top games for free play throughout the day, from 11am to 8pm. In addition, we will be hosting multiple tournaments throughout the day. Gaming will be located in the Price Center East Ballroom, ERC College Room, Marshall College Room, and Revelle College Room.
