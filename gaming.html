Gaming

We have video games and table top games for free play from 11am to 8pm. In addition, we will be hosting tournaments throughout the day. Gaming will be located in the Price Center West Ballroom. There will also be Game Shows happening on the mainstage and Muir room.

Tournament Descriptions

All tournaments are held in PC West Ballroom. Sign-ups will be held at the Gaming Table in PC West next to all of the game set-ups. Sign-ups for each tournament will start at 11am and will close 15 minutes before their respective tournaments. (For BlazBlue Cross Tag Battle, we will be holding online pre-registration starting on February 8.) After signing up, please check in at the Gaming Table 10 minutes before the tournament start time. If sign-ups go over the cap, there will be a waitlist.
