2020 Gaming

We have video games for free play from 11:00am to 1:30pm and 6:30pm-8:00pm. In addition, we will be hosting tournaments throughout the day. Gaming will be located in the Sun God Lounge above PC Theater. There will also be Game Shows happening within the convention.
In addition, we have table top games and game shows throughout the day.

Tournament Descriptions

All tournaments are held in Sun God Lounge above PC Theater. Sign-ups will be held online, but can also be done at the Gaming Table in PC West next to all of the game set-ups if necessary. Sign-ups for each tournament will start at 10am and will close 30 minutes before their respective tournaments.

Smash Ultimate Tournament

1:30pm - 4:30pm, 5:30pm - 6:30pm

Max Entrants: 128

Format: Double Elimination

Sign-ups, 10:00am - 1:00pm

Looking to smash with others in the San Diego area? Drop by the gaming setups for a near convention-long Smash Ultimate tournament with some major prizes in store. Each tournament setup will have two preset nicknames with tap jump disabled and stick sensitivity high (named AF-1 and AF-2).
