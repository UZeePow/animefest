Milky Cannon

Jose Mendoza - Guitar
Khang Mach - Bass
Anusith Sounnadath - Drums

Anyone familiar with the format of Japanese music singles would know that they often contain a song and an instrumental version of that same song. We like to think of ourselves as representatives of the latter portion of the single. This is not so much an organized band, but rather a collaboration between friends who enjoy Anime but also look forward to the openings and endings that were created for each show. There's a web of musical interplay that is often ignored due to the vocal emphasis in Anime music and we hope to shed some light on the music that accompanies the singing. Listeners will surely hear songs, old and new, from a different perspective and performed by fans, for fans.
