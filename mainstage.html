Mainstage Event / Performance Descriptions

G-Moto

11:30am-12:00pm

G-Moto combines the techniques of blowing air into the 篠笛/shinobue (Japanese bamboo flute) while beatboxing at the same time to covers from anime and video games, along with original music. Beginning in early 2018, G-Moto has performed at various cultural festivals and anime/video game conventions, such as Anime Expo, the largest anime convention in North America which has had over 100,000+ attendees in 2019. In 2020, G-Moto was a Finalist at Anime Los Angeles’ Next Top Star talent competition and won the Honorable Mention Award. G-Moto can be followed on Instagram at @g.moto.fbx

Intermission Orchestra at UCSD

1:00pm-1:30pm

The Intermission Orchestra is a student-run organization dedicated to arranging and playing music from anime, video games, and films. What was background music is now the feature.

Setlist:

  • Il Vento D'oro from Jojo's Bizarre Adventure: Golden Wind
  • Tanjiro's Song from Demon Slayer
  • Isabelle's Lullaby from The Promised Neverland


Cosplay Masquerade

2:00pm-2:30pm

A twist on the classic "costume contest." Come dressed as your favorite characters and be judged accordingly. Since the audience votes for the winner, you can curry favor through spirit, elegance, kawaii, entertainment value, or with whatever angle you want within reason.
