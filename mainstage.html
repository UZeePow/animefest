Weakest Link

11:30pm to 12:30pm

The famous British game show now has a Japanese twist! Test your quiz-show mettle with quickfire general knowledge questions related to Japan or otaku culture to add into a shared prize pool. Contestants will vote off the "weakest link" until only two remain, and the winner of the showdown will be crowned winner as the Strongest Link and takes the plunders of the prize pool. Do you have what it takes? (Total contestants limited to 6-9)

Intermission Orchestra at UCSD

2:00pm - 2:30pm

The Intermission Orchestra is a student-run organization dedicated to arranging and playing music from anime, video games, and films. What was background music is now the feature.

Setlist:

  • Hacking to the Gate
  • Kaze ni Naru
  • Lacrimosa
  • Legend of Ashitaka
  • Nausicaa of the Valley of the Wind


Cosplay Masquerade

3:00pm - 4:00pm

A twist on the classic "costume contest." Come dressed as your favorite characters and be judged accordingly. Since the audience votes for the winner, you can curry favor through spirit, elegance, kawaii, entertainment value, or with whatever angle you want within reason.

If You Are the One

4:00pm - 5:00pm

Want to see crack ships be formed in real time? Join us in roleplaying our favorite anime characters or character types as we find to help them achieve true love! This well-known Chinese game show a suitor has to impress a panel of up to 24 (single) men or women with a series of short videos and Q&A sessions, with the hopes that they can end the show with a first date. (unlimited contestants allowed).
