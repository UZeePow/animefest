Main Stage

The Animefest main stage is the location of all our large, interactive events. The main stage is located in Price Center West Ballroom.

Mainstage Event / Performance Descriptions

Cosplay Masquerade

12:00pm - 1:45pm

Masquerade! Cosplayers on parade... Masquerade! Hide your obsession, so reality will never find you!

A twist on the classic "costume contest." Come dressed as your favorite characters and be judged accordingly. Since the audience votes for the winner, you can curry favor through spirit, elegance, kawaii, entertainment value, or with whatever angle you want within reason.

dan(k)ce weebs

1:45pm - 1:50pm

We're a small group of friends repping various dance teams from the UCSD Dance Community. We're here to bring some major energy to the stage this year. Come watch our performance, you won't regret it.

Intermission Orchestra at UCSD

2:30pm - 3:00pm

The Intermission Orchestra is a student-run organization dedicated to playing and arranging music from anime, video games, and films. Come enjoy a few hours of your favorite music at our quarterly concerts! This quarter’s concert is on March 10, 2018, in PC Ballroom West, from 8 PM – 10 PM. Catch us at Animefest and the Winter GameFest this quarter as well!

Open Mic Karaoke

6pm - 8pm

Take the stage and sing your blessed little hearts out! Open to top idols and the tone-deaf alike, come perform your favorite songs with your friends or fly solo. Let loose and enjoy a time of song and maybe some dance sequences.
