Events

Anime Quiplash

11am - 12:30pm

Grab your smartphones, computers, or other devices for a game that is basically an open-ended Cards Against Humanity. Majority rules with this game! Provide your answers to anime related questions, and collect points based on how many people voted for what you wrote. While there is only room for eight players per round, there is unlimited space for audience members who can sway the game.

Anime Jeopardy

12:30pm - 1:30pm

Test the depths of your weeaboo knowledge in this anime themed classic. Feel the intense emotions of Jeopardy: the frustration of grasping at an answer that is on the tip of your tongue, the despair of having no idea what the answer could be, or the pride of achieving victory in the final round. Put your years (or recent fondness) of otaku culture to use and discover what you know (or do not know) about anime.

Cosplay Masquerade

2:30pm - 4pm

Masquerade! Cosplayers on parade... Masquerade! Hide your obsession, so reality will never find you!

A twist on the classic "costume contest." Come dressed as your favorite characters and be judged accordingly. Since the audience votes for the winner, you can curry favor through spirit, elegance, kawaii, entertainment value, or with whatever angle you want within reason.

Open Mic Karaoke

6pm - 8pm

Take the stage and sing your blessed little hearts out! Open to top idols and the tone-deaf alike, come perform your favorite songs with your friends or fly solo. Let loose and enjoy a time of song and maybe some dance sequences.
