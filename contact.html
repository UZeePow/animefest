About Cal Animage Beta

Cal Animage Beta is UC San Diego's anime club. We hold weekly meetings on Mondays, where we show anime or hold other fun events. We also have other socials on weekends, such as karaoke, game nights, potlucks, and Animefest! Membership is open to all!

If you are interested in learning more about our club or events, please visit our website ucsd.moe for more information. Alternatively, check out our Facebook group here, or contact us through the email above.
