Panel Descriptions

Marshall Room: Game Show Panels

Jeopardy

12:00pm - 1:00pm

Do you like game shows? Want a chance at some raffle tickets and maybe some prizes? Then come and test your weeb knowledge with our very own anime Jeopardy!

Wheel of Fortune

1:00pm - 2:30pm

TBA

The Price is Right (Anime Edition)

2:30pm - 3:30pm

Earn the chance to win more raffle tickets in this event, inspired by the popular TV game show of the same name! Form a group and compete with others to see who can best appraise various anime/Japanese goods and merchandise!

Guess the Anime Based on the Badly Written Premise

4:30pm - 5:30pm

Can you recognize an anime based on its summary? Use your advanced weeb skills to identify a variety of shows using only a description that almost sounds like something else entirely.

Name That Anime Song!

6:00pm - 7:00pm

Test your knowledge of anime theme songs, OSTs, Openings, and Endings! Participants will be divided into teams and tackle an array of miscellaneous songs from across anime history. Listen to the song of your heart, and failing that, listen to the song of someone else's heart. You're on a team, after all.
