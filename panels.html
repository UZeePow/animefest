Panel Descriptions

Marshall Room: Student/Alumni Panels

Collegiate Gaming Communities (CGCs) by Tale of Kenji

11:00am - 12:00pm

This presentation will cover an overview of Collegiate Gaming Communities (CGCs) at colleges and universities to provide a historical and current context for this growing student population. This event will also host a networking social for current/aspiring student leaders involved in CGCs to meet fellow CGC members. This is intended for attendees to cultivate new professional connections and learn from each other on how to advance their existing clubs/organizations or potentially start new ones.

Figure Collecting 101 by Jesus Guerra Fernatt Jr.

12:00pm - 1:00pm

An introduction to the figure collecting hobby, overview of the various figure making companies, and how to go about buying figures. In this panel we will discuss some of the upcoming figures from many of the top figure manufacturers as well as giving a general summary of the top companies work. We will also discuss things to look for in figures and how to avoid getting bootlegs.

Anime and You: Welcome to the Good Life by Jericho Cabanayan

5:00pm - 6:00pm

Being an anime fan is hard work. When we discover anime, we do one of two things: keep watching it or don't watch it ever again. If we keep watching it though, there's the possibility that we might reach otaku enlightenment by pursuing things that show our devotion to the medium such as cosplay, figure collecting, or even rapping. Nonetheless, even if we embrace the anime subculture, there will always be a tension between us and the people both inside and outside of the community. Join Jericho Cabanayan, the legendary harem master that clawed his way up to otaku enlightenment, as he discusses the life, pain, and romance of being a weeaboo during the early 2000s to now. Featuring special guests such as Jericho's best waifus and husbandos, this is going to be an unforgettable panel experience.
