Panel Descriptions

Marshall Room: Game Show Panels

Jeopardy

12:00pm - 1:00pm

Do you like game shows? Want a chance at some raffle tickets and maybe some prizes? Then come and test your weeb knowledge with our very own anime Jeopardy!

Hangman Wonderland (Wheel of Fortune Anime Edition)

1:00pm - 2:00pm

Guess hidden phrases by guessing letters one at a time. Win raffle tickets, determined by a spin of the wheel, for each round you win. But you have to pay to see what vowels are in a puzzle. The team that has amassed the most points in a round wins!

The Price is Right (Anime Edition)

2:30pm - 3:30pm

Earn the chance to win more raffle tickets in this event, inspired by the popular TV game show of the same name! Form a group and compete with others to see who can best appraise various anime/Japanese goods and merchandise!

Guess the Anime Based on the Badly Written Premise

4:30pm - 5:30pm

Can you recognize an anime based on its summary? Use your advanced weeb skills to identify a variety of shows using only a description that almost sounds like something else entirely.

ERC Room

Your Waifu Can Hack Into Your Heart but Not IRL by Chris Mcguire

11:30am - 12:30pm

Ever watch anime and come across the occasional hacker in your favorite Action Sci-fi or thousandth Isekai? Fascinated by how computers and various tech works? Then, there's a pretty good chance you share a geeky mind like ours. So, come join us as we revisit various anime throughout the decade praising or roasting some beloved anime characters.

Name That Anime Song!

1:30pm - 2:30pm

Test your knowledge of anime theme songs, OSTs, Openings, and Endings! Participants will be divided into teams and tackle an array of miscellaneous songs from across anime history. Listen to the song of your heart, and failing that, listen to the song of someone else's heart. You're on a team, after all.

Oni Time: A Retrospective of Oni and Their Representation Throughout Anime

2:30pm - 3:30pm

Learn about the history and representation of Oni throughout history and anime!

A Comprehensive Guide to the Madoka Magica Franchise

3:30pm - 4:30pm

Puella Magi Madoka Magica is one of the most critically-acclaimed and influential anime of the past decade. Nine years after the series first aired, the franchise is still extremely popular among anime communities, with a myriad of movies, spin-offs, games, and other supplementary materials for fans to enjoy. This panel serves as a guide for anyone who enjoyed Madoka Magica and wants to explore the franchise further. It will describe each work in the franchise, the watching/reading orders of those works, and the availability of those works in the United States. There will be spoilers for the original series, but not for other works.

The Art of the Weeb Flex: A Guide to Anime Merch

6:00pm - 7:00pm

There's a lot of different ways to express your love of anime, from humble keychains to life-sized figurines. What kind of merch is right for me? Where can I buy these things? Join us on a safari tour through the world of anime merchandise, both official and fanmade, and learn about all the quality life decisions you can make to flex your weeb power level. Some of the topics I'll cover in this panel includes scale figures, Blu-Ray sets, and itasha.
