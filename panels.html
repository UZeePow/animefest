Panel Descriptions

Marshall Room

From Astolfo to Nitori Shuuichi - An Academic Review of Transgender Characters in Japanese Pop Culture by Brian Lu Nguyen

12:00pm - 1:00pm

An academic review of trans characters in Japanese anime, manga, and video games, evaluating their representation and framing on a case-by-case basis. Alternative, clickbait titles include: “Are Traps Gay?” and “The Gender Hideyoshi".

Hayao Miyazaki Presents: The Panel for Antisocial Weebs You Know You Need Tactical media recommending operations by Alfonso and Jesus

1:00pm - 2:00pm

A panel about how to recommend media to another person. This can range from how to do it, how not to do it, and even the expectations of what recommending media does to others.

Comparison of Various Mobile Game (Mobage) Models by David Li

2:30pm - 3:30pm

A panel on comparing the various game and payment models of popular mobile games over time. Key milestones are: Puzzle and Dragons (stamina model), Kantai Collection (resource model), Granblue Fantasy, Pokemon Go, Fate/Grand Order (and variants), Girls' Frontline, Azur Lane and other non-Japanese mobage.

My First Serious Animefest Panel by frosty

3:30pm - 4:30pm

Familiarity with Linux is a prerequisite. You can also just show up to meme.

How To Die From Anime Merchandise by Thiago Marback

6:00pm - 7:00pm

Did you just finish a great series and want to support the studio beyond just not pirating it? Want to decorate your entire room with your waifu(s)/husbando(s)? Or just really hate having money in your bank account? Then this panel is for you! We'll be doing an overview of the different kinds of otaku merchandise money can buy, from keychains to itashas, and some of the places where you can burn your money away instead of spending it on silly things like basic necessities.

Revelle Room

Alternate Fates: Chronological and Broadcast Watch Orders by Marshall and Carlos

1:00pm - 2:00pm

Join Marshall and Carlos as they discuss the potential benefits and drawbacks of the two main watch orders of the main timeline of the Fate franchise.

Figure Collecting 101 by Jesus Fematt Guerra Jr

2:30pm - 3:30pm

Have you ever wanted to get into world of figure collecting? Not sure how? This panel works out as a great starting point for the hobby! We'll have an overview of many of the popular figure maker companies, go over some technical terms, and we'll go over how to procure figures. We'll cover how to identify and avoid figures so that you can support the anime industry and acquire a legitimate product. Great for newcomers and experienced collectors!

Poorly Explaining the Plot of Touhou: A Primer by Duck Life

3:30pm - 4:30pm

Ever been curious about what the Touhou series is all about? Well you've come to the wrong place! Here, I will try my best to unravel the convoluted and insane story of the Touhou games, as well as give insight as to its history and reasons for its popularity. Just what is a Touhou? Why are there doujins and fan games everywhere? And why is it's music so good? Find out all that and more!

Karuta v2.0 Free Play

6:00pm - 7:00pm

Anime music meets traditional Japanese card game in Anime OP/ED Karuta. Standard karuta rules apply, but now with a playlist of openings and endings from a variety of anime series. Karuta’s third appearance at Animefest comes with a new change – 80 card decks and a host of new songs. Only 50 cards will be on the board at any given time, so be wary of looking for a song that isn’t there.

Muir Room: Game Show Panels

Midday Dub Fight

12:00pm - 1:00pm

Have mad voice acting skills? Have perfect comedic timing and ability to adapt and overcome any scenario? Whether you are horribly good or beautifully awful at voice acting, join us for Midday Dub Fight! Teams will be assigned a random, unsubtitled, mystery anime clip to dub over. Teams will first watch the clip, then be allowed a moment to prepare a story and characterization. Over that, teams will present their scene to the audience. All participants will be awarded in raffle tickets.

Pointless

1:00pm - 2:00pm

The one game where the lowest score wins! Come join with a buddy and try to use the power of otaku knowledge to find the most obscure answers to questions relating to anime and manga. Those who can get a pointless answer will get a special prize (total contestants limited to 4 pairs).

Whose Sub is it Anyway

2:30pm - 3:30pm

Think you know Japanese well enough to make anime subs? Me neither, but Crunchyroll does. Guess which anime subtitle is the "official" (read: Crunchyroll) translation for a variety of anime scenes. Each scene will be played without subs for around 5 to 10 seconds, then players will have a minute to guess which subtitle among four is correct.

Name that Anime Song!

3:30pm - 4:30pm

Test your knowledge of anime theme songs, OSTs, Openings, and Endings! Participants will be divided into teams and tackle an array of miscellaneous songs from across anime history. Listen to the song of your heart, and failing that, listen to the song of someone else's heart. You're on a team, after all.
