Location

Animefest is held in Price Center on UC San Diego's campus. Specifically, our events will be held in the Price Center West and East Ballrooms, Price Center Theater, and the ERC, Marshall, Muir, and Revelle College rooms.

Parking

Parking is free all day on Sundays on UCSD's campus. We recommend you park in the Gilman Parking Structure (corner of Russell Lane and Gilman Drive), as it is the closest parking area to Price Center.
